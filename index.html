MiNotes

Local-first Markdown notes. Your files stay on your disk — no server, no account, fully offline.

This browser doesn't support opening folders from disk (File System Access API), so MiNotes will keep your notes in a private browser-managed workspace instead. For direct access to .md files on disk, use Chrome or Edge on desktop.

  • Markdown files — plain .md you can open anywhere
  • [[Wikilinks]] — bidirectional links between notes
  • Instant search — fuzzy, phrase and boolean, all on-device
  • Sync via your OS — pick a Drive/Dropbox/OneDrive folder and it syncs itself
