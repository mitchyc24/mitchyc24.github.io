Welcome to My Chess Game!

Hello and welcome to my implementation of chess! This game was built leveraging the Chess.js and Chessboard.js libraries, and generated custom chess piece designs using OpenAI's DALL-E 2 image generator. Each piece has been crafted to provide a unique and visually appealing chess experience.

Currently there is no computer opponent, I am working on training my own AI model to eventually play agaisnt my website visitors. My goal is to be able to train a chess AI that can eventually outplay me!
